Here is a flowchart representation of your program's workflow:

- **Directory Scanner**: Scans the "Vault Directory" for files.
- **Main Controller**: Manages tasks and sends files to the File Processor.
- **File Processor**: Cleans, chunks, and processes files.
- **LLM Client**: Handles communication with Ollama and generates embeddings.
- **Ollama**: Provides LLM-based responses used for further processing.
- **ChromaDB**: Stores embeddings for later retrieval.
